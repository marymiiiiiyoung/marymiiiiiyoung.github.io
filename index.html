Welcome

Hello! I'm Miyoung Chung

Thank you for visiting my Website. I am a graduate student, a Masters' degree candidate, in UNIST(Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology). Currently, I'm a member of BCILAB, guided by Dr.SungPhil Kim.

I am interested in music and speech BCI(Brain-computer interface) development and the neural mechanism of those. Lately, I've developed a real-time musical neuro-feedback system, BrainCoder, an instrument with the brain. If you want to see the system and mechanism related to it, check out Project and CV tab on the menu bar.

Check out About Tab and you will find more fun factors of me!
