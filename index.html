Welcome

Hello! I'm Miyoung Chung

Thank you for visiting my Website. I am a doctorate student for Neuroscience at McGill University in Canada, from the fall of 2022. From 2022 Fall Semester, I became a member of The Auditory Cognitive Neuroscience Laboratory, guided by Dr.Zatorre. I've received my Master's Degree for Biomedical Engineering at Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology (UNIST) and was a member of BCILAB

I am interested in the neural mechanism of music and speech, especially in pitch sound perception in their context. If you want to see the works I've done, check out Project and CV tab on the menu bar.

Check out About Tab and you will find more about me!
